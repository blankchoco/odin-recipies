Grilled Cheese Sandwich

Description

Ingredients

  • 4 slices white bread
  • 3 tablespoons butter, divided
  • 2 slices Cheddar cheese

Steps

  1. Gather all the ingredients.
  2. Preheat a nonstick skillet over medium heat. Generously butter one side of each bread slice.
  3. Place 1 bread slice, butter-side down, in the hot skillet; top with 1 cheese slice.
  4. Place the other bread slice, butter-side up, on top of the cheese slice.
  5. Cook until lightly browned on one side; flip and continue cooking until the cheese is melted.
  6. Repeat with the remaining bread and cheese slices. Serve and enjoy!
